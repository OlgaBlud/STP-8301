About This Game

Toy Match 3D: Triple Match is an exciting puzzle adventure where you match, sort, and collect adorable 3D toy objects. Developed by Glowstorm Studios, this game is designed to train your mind, challenge your skills, and provide a fun, engaging experience. Find and pop up identical toys, clear the board, and advance through unique levels with increasing difficulty. If you love puzzle-solving and relaxing gameplay, Toy Match 3D is perfect for you!

How to Play

  • Match Triple Objects

    Find and tap three identical 3D toys to clear them.

  • Clear the Board

    Match all objects before running out of space.
