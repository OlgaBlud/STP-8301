How to Play

  • Match Triple Objects

    Find and tap three identical 3D toys to clear them.

  • Clear the Board

    Match all objects before running out of space.

  • Use Power-Ups

    Utilize boosters to help with challenging levels.

  • Progress Through Levels

    Unlock new puzzles and toy collections as you advance.

  • Enjoy Offline Play

    Have fun anytime, even without an internet connection.
